Traffic Congestion Prediction Using Data Forecasting

Predicting urban mobility patterns using advanced Machine Learning techniques to reduce commute times and improve city planning.

Get Started

The Challenge

Traffic congestion is a major problem in urban areas, leading to increased fuel consumption, pollution, and lost productivity. Traditional traffic management systems often fail to adapt to real-time changes.

Our Solution

By leveraging historical traffic data and AI-based forecasting, our system provides accurate predictions of congestion levels. This allows commuters and city authorities to make informed decisions.

How it Works

We use Time Series Forecasting and Regression models to analyze patterns in vehicle volume, weather conditions, and time of day to forecast future traffic states.
